Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Stage 3 test?

expand_more

The Stage 3 test is our most comprehensive DNA analysis, examining over 500,000 genetic markers to provide deep insights into your health, wellness, ancestry, and carrier status for certain inherited conditions.

How accurate are the results?

expand_more

Our lab uses state-of-the-art genotyping technology, achieving over 99% accuracy for the markers we test. However, it's important to remember these are informational and not diagnostic tests.

What do the reports cover?

expand_more

You'll receive detailed reports covering your genetic predisposition for various health traits, personalized wellness recommendations for diet and exercise, a breakdown of your ancestral origins, and your carrier status for select conditions.
