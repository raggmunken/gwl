Frequently Asked Questions

How do I interpret my results? expand_more

Your results will be available in your online portal within 2-3 weeks of sample receipt. You'll receive an email notification when they're ready. The report will provide detailed insights into your genetic predispositions, along with personalized recommendations for diet, exercise, and lifestyle adjustments.

What is the turnaround time for results? expand_more

Typically, you can expect your results within 2-3 weeks from the time our lab receives your sample. We will send you an email notification as soon as your personalized report is ready to be viewed in your secure online portal.
